# B.U.L. Bylaws Summary (v2019-09-16)

Full document: `Don't touch_ Barrie Ultimate Leage Bylaws v20190916.docx`

## Organization Purpose
- Organize Ultimate leagues
- Teach, develop, and promote Ultimate
- Foster goodwill and sportsmanship per Spirit of the Game

## Head Office
Located in Barrie area, at location determined by Board.

## Membership
- Open to anyone (or parent/guardian for under-18) who participates in a league/event, pays dues, and signs waivers
- Members may resign at any time
- Board can discipline members (including suspension/expulsion) with majority Board vote; member has right to be heard

## Board of Directors
- **Size**: 3–10 Directors
- **Election**: Direct vote at AGM, majority of members present
- **Age requirement**: 18+, member in good standing
- **Powers**: Administer affairs, authorize expenditures, execute contracts
- **Compensation**: None (but reimbursed for expenses including registration fees)
- **Meetings**: Min 3/year, 48hr notice, majority = quorum, 1 vote per director
- **Removal**: Recall election within 90 days of petition signed by 5% of members
- **Vacancy**: New directors appointed by 2/3 majority of directors, ratified at next AGM

## Officers & Terms

| Role | Term |
|------|------|
| President | 2 years |
| Vice-President | 2 years (1 year if elected same year as President) |
| Treasurer | 1 year |
| Secretary | 1 year |
| Communications Director | 1 year |
| Leagues Director | 1 year |
| Other Officers/Agents (incl. Equipment Director, Spirit Coordinator) | Up to 2 years or as determined by Board majority |

### President
- Chief executive officer
- Chairs all Board and membership meetings
- Casting vote on ties

### Vice-President
- Acts as President in their absence
- Other duties as assigned by Board

### Treasurer
- Custody of all funds
- Maintains bank account(s)
- Full accounting records
- Renders financial statements to Board/membership

### Secretary
- Gives notice of meetings
- Keeps: Articles of Incorporation, bylaws, resolutions, member register, director register, meeting minutes

### Communications Director
- Website, social media, email communications
- Assists with registration coordination

### Leagues Director
- Oversees management of all leagues

### Other Officers/Agents (Equipment Director, etc.)
- Appointed by Board as needed
- Authority and duties prescribed by Board
- Must act honestly and in good faith

## Meetings

### Annual General Meeting (AGM)
- Held yearly in Barrie area
- 2 weeks notice via website + member notification
- **Quorum**: 10 members (in person or proxy)
- Proxy voting allowed (proxyholder must be a member)
- Each member gets 1 vote
- Board presents financial position

### Special General Meeting
- Called by 10% of members or Executive Director
- 2 weeks written notice with proxy reminder

## Key Policies
- **League activities** (new season, etc.): Approved by Board majority
- **Temporary policy changes**: Board majority
- **Permanent policy changes**: Majority of members at General Meeting
- **Bylaw amendments**: 2/3 vote at General Meeting; any member may propose

## Indemnification
- Organization indemnifies Directors and Officers (and heirs/executors) from claims arising from their duties
- **Exception**: No indemnification for fraud, dishonesty, or bad faith

## Dissolution
- Remaining funds/assets distributed to incorporated non-profit Canadian organization
- Requires Board determination + 2/3 member ratification
