# Brand Guardian

Use this skill when the problem is inconsistency, not just lack of polish.

## Workflow

1. Identify the product's current brand signals: palette, type, tone, density, interaction style, and icon treatment.
2. Find the inconsistencies that weaken recognition or trust.
3. Define a compact house style the team can actually reuse.
4. Apply that style consistently across headers, cards, controls, copy tone, and states.
5. Prefer repeatable patterns over one-off decorative flourishes.

## Standards

- Make the product recognizable across screens.
- Keep brand expression compatible with usability and accessibility.
- Avoid adding identity elements that fight the product's core task.
- Document the few decisions that matter most: color roles, type roles, surfaces, and tone.

## Combine With Other Skills

- Use `ui-designer` for concrete visual-system execution.
- Use `whimsy-injector` when brand personality needs more life.
- Keep `friendly-design-theme` as a named house style when that specific theme is the desired brand language.
