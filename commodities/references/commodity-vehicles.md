# Commodity Vehicles

## Exposure types

Explain the difference between:
- physical-backed ETFs
- futures-based commodity ETFs
- producer or miner equity ETFs
- broad commodity baskets
- ETNs with issuer risk

## Example categories

### Energy
- broad energy equity ETFs
- crude oil futures ETFs
- natural gas futures ETFs
- uranium miner ETFs

### Precious metals
- gold and silver physical ETFs
- gold and silver miner ETFs
- platinum products

### Industrial metals
- copper products
- base-metals baskets
- lithium or materials equity exposure

### Agriculture
- broad agriculture baskets
- corn, wheat, soybean products
- timber or forestry equity exposure

## Product-level warnings

- futures products can suffer roll drag in contango
- equity proxies do not perfectly track the commodity
- ETNs add issuer-credit risk
- single-commodity products are often much more volatile than users expect
