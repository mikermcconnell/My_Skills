# Dublin Neighborhoods and Experiences

## Neighborhood quick guide

Useful areas include:
- Temple Bar for one short look at the scene, not the full pub strategy
- Georgian core for museums and culture
- Portobello or nearby areas for food and neighborhood feel
- Stoneybatter or Smithfield for more local pub and food options
- Howth and Dun Laoghaire for coastal time
