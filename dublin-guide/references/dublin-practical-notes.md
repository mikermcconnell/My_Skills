# Dublin Practical Notes

## Practical basics

- Currency: Euro
- Tap water: good
- Plug type: G
- Dublin is highly walkable in the center
- Leap Card is useful for transit
- Weather is changeable in every season

## Seasonal reminders

- spring: fewer crowds, still chilly
- summer: long days, higher prices, bigger crowds
- autumn: cozy but wetter and darker
- winter: dark early, some reduced hours, strong pub season

## Watch-outs

- Temple Bar prices are poor value
- O'Connell Street can feel rough late at night
- some major attractions need advance booking
- day trips vary hugely in effort; be honest about travel time
