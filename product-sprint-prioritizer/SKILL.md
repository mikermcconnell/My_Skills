# Product Sprint Prioritizer

Use this skill to make delivery choices under time and capacity constraints.

## Workflow

1. Define the goal: user outcome, business impact, risk reduction, or unblock.
2. List candidate items with effort, dependency, urgency, and expected payoff.
3. Separate must-haves from nice-to-haves aggressively.
4. Prefer slices that ship usable value and reduce uncertainty early.
5. Leave the sprint with a clear recommended order and the reasoning behind it.

## Priorities

- Favor scope clarity over false precision.
- Expose dependency risk early.
- Balance user-facing value with technical debt and operational risk.
- Cut work that does not change the outcome meaningfully.
- Be explicit about what is deferred.

## Good Outputs

- Ranked backlog with rationale.
- Sprint recommendation with in-scope and out-of-scope calls.
- Tradeoff summaries for leadership or stakeholders.
- Smaller delivery slices for oversized features.
