# Confidence and Source Rules

## Confidence levels

Use simple levels such as:
- High: multiple quality sources or primary evidence align
- Medium: some support but gaps remain
- Low: single-source, contradictory, or weak evidence

## Source quality reminders

Prefer:
- primary documents
- official documentation
- peer-reviewed work
- high-quality reporting with named sourcing

Use more caution with:
- aggregators
- unattributed summaries
- social media threads
- forum anecdotes

## Common traps

- echo chamber repetition
- outdated articles being reused as current
- opinion framed as fact
- one strong chart standing in for the whole story
