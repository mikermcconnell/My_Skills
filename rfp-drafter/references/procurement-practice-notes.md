# Procurement Practice Notes

## Transit-specific considerations

Useful requirements can include:
- municipal transit planning experience
- Ontario funding familiarity
- AODA awareness
- electrification, on-demand, or fare-integration experience when relevant

## Timeline guidance

Allow enough time for:
- questions period
- addenda
- proposal preparation
- evaluation and interviews if used
- negotiation and award steps

## Common pitfalls

Watch for:
- unclear deliverables
- evaluation criteria that don't match the stated priorities
- unrealistic timelines
- inconsistent pricing instructions
- requirements that screen out strong firms without good reason
