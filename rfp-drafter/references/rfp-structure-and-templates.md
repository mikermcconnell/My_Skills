# RFP Structure and Templates

## Common document types

- RFP for complex qualitative procurements
- RFQ for price-led, well-defined work
- pre-qualification when shortlisting first
- standing offer for recurring work

## Standard structure

Typical sections:
1. introduction and background
2. scope of work
3. proposal requirements
4. evaluation criteria
5. terms and conditions
6. submission instructions
7. appendices

## Scope template

A good scope usually includes:
- objectives
- deliverables
- tasks and phases
- optional services if needed
- out-of-scope items

## Evaluation template

Typical criteria buckets:
- corporate experience
- team
- methodology
- work plan
- pricing

## Pricing template

Common elements:
- fees by phase or task
- hourly rates by role
- disbursements
- tax treatment
- total price summary
