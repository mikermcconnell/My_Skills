# Risk Register Templates

## Risk register structure

A useful register usually includes:
- last updated
- owner
- review frequency
- summary dashboard
- individual risk entries

## Probability scale

Use a consistent 1-5 scale from rare to almost certain.

## Impact scale

Use a consistent 1-5 scale with practical descriptions.
Common lenses:
- financial impact
- service impact
- reputational impact

## Individual risk entry template

Each risk should include:
- ID and title
- category
- owner
- date identified
- status
- description
- inherent and residual scoring
- root causes
- consequences
- mitigation strategies
- monitoring indicators
- contingency plan
- history

## Reporting template

Periodic risk reporting should usually include:
- risk status summary
- top risks
- risks requiring attention
- emerging risks
- risks closed
