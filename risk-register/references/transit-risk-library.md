# Transit Risk Library

## Common transit strategic-plan risks

### Strategic
- ridership recovery shortfall
- competitive mode shift
- regional integration failure

### Operational
- operator recruitment and retention
- fleet reliability issues
- capacity constraints
- on-demand demand mismatch

### Financial
- funding reduction
- capital or electrification overruns
- fare revenue underperformance

### External
- recession effects
- boundary or growth pressure
- climate disruption
- supply chain delays

## Mitigation types

Common types:
- avoid
- reduce
- transfer
- accept
- exploit

## Use this library carefully

Use these as starting points, not canned outputs.
Adapt them to the specific project, timeframe, and operating environment.
