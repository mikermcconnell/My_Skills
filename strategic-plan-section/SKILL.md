## Writing Style Guide

### Tone
- **Confident but realistic** - Aspirational goals with credible paths
- **Accessible language** - Minimize jargon, define when necessary
- **Action-oriented** - Focus on what will be done, not just analyzed

### Formatting Conventions
- Use tables for comparisons and data
- Include "why it matters" context for each initiative
- Show baselines and targets for all metrics
- Reference source documents where applicable

### Key Messages to Reinforce
- Ridership growth as primary goal
- Budget-conscious approach with strong business cases
- Building on recent successes (network transformation, electrification)
- Regional integration and GO coordination
- AI/innovation leadership opportunity

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any section:

- [ ] Aligns with ridership growth primary goal
- [ ] Includes measurable targets with baselines
- [ ] Connects to City Strategic Plan priorities
- [ ] Acknowledges budget constraints
- [ ] References peer best practices where relevant
- [ ] Written in accessible, jargon-free language
- [ ] Tables and visuals enhance understanding
- [ ] Actions have clear ownership and timing
