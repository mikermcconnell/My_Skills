## Core section templates

### Executive Summary
Include:
- opportunity statement
- strategic direction
- key initiatives
- expected outcomes
- investment summary

### Vision, Mission & Values
Include:
- vision
- mission
- values with short explanations
- guiding principles

### Strategic Goals & Pillars
Include:
- goal framework
- each pillar
- KPIs with baseline and target where available
- strategic initiatives

### Service Plan
Include:
- network evolution by phase
- family-of-services model
- service standards
- growth triggers

### Financial Plan
Include:
- operating budget projections
- capital funding strategy
- scenario framing
- affordability or subsidy implications

### Implementation Roadmap
Include:
- year-by-year phasing
- decision points
- quick wins
- dependencies
