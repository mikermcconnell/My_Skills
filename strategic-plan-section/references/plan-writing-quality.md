# Plan Writing Quality

## Writing style

- Use plain, professional language.
- Keep the writing strategic, not academic.
- Use tables when comparing options, phases, targets, or funding needs.
- Make implementation implications visible.

## Quality checklist

Before finishing, check:
- Does the section match the approved plan structure?
- Is the strategic purpose clear in the first few lines?
- Are timelines, phasing, or targets visible where they should be?
- Are recommendations practical and tied to delivery reality?
- Is the wording consistent with Barrie Transit planning language?
