# Whimsy Injector

Use this skill to add personality after the product already works.

## Workflow

1. Identify where the product feels flat: empty states, confirmations, hover states, errors, onboarding, or success moments.
2. Add a small number of deliberate delight moments instead of scattering novelty everywhere.
3. Keep every flourish functional: orientation, reassurance, momentum, or brand memory.
4. Respect reduced-motion, accessibility, and performance constraints.
5. Stop before the product feels cute at the expense of clarity.

## Good Targets

- Empty states that need warmth or motivation.
- Success and error messages that need more personality.
- Hover, press, and loading states that feel dead.
- Product areas that need memorability without a full redesign.

## Combine With Other Skills

- Use `ui-designer` for the core visual system.
- Use `brand-guardian` to keep playful choices on-brand.
- Use `friendly-design-theme` when the delight should still sit inside the established warm operational style.
